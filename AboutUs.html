Our Story

J&J was founded by two brothers, Jonathan and Jacob Jamesworth. Ever since they were six years old, they loved creating and eating pastry.

This led them to create J&J, one of the most popular bakeries in The Big Apple. The two brothers work tirelessly to create the most declious pastry this world has to offer.

All of us at J&J hope our delicious pastries brighten up your day!
